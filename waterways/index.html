Florida Waterways


Link to high resolution map

Authored by: Brian Shelton

This map was created for HydroTime to display Florida stream networks. Streams are categorized based on the Strahler Stream Order system. Map may be viewed in both low resolution (1200px) and high resolution (8000px). Date created: February 2019. Coordinate system used: NAD83/Florida GDL Albers. Data was acquired from: The National Map Small-Scale website.
